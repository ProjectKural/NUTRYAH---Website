FAQs

How does the system handle ambulance coordination?

Ambulances can be tracked in real time with ETA, crew allocation, and incoming patient alerts synced to the ED dashboard.

Is the system NABH compliant?

Yes. All emergency workflows align with NABH standards, including handover protocols, documentation, and MLC handling.

Can emergency cases be handed over to IPD or ICU directly?

Yes. Our system supports structured handover notes with real-time bed availability and condition tracking.

Does the platform support casualty case classification?

Yes. Casualty types (accidental, poisoning, trauma, etc.) can be tagged and filtered for compliance and analytics.
