FAQs

Can I integrate ICU devices like monitors and ventilators?

Yes. Nutryah supports integration with common bedside monitors and ventilators for live vital streaming and charting.

Are clinical alerts configurable?

Absolutely. You can define alert thresholds and escalation levels for vitals like BP, SpO2, HR, and temperature.

Is device data stored with timestamps?

Yes. All connected devices log time-series data and can be reviewed in trend charts or exported for analysis.

Can staff handovers be audited?

Yes. Each ICU handover is timestamped, logged by role, and archived for compliance review.
