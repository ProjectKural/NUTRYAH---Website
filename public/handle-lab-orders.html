FAQs

Can I define lab workflows by department?

Yes. Biochemistry, Hematology, Microbiology, etc. can have separate test panels and routing rules.

How are rejected samples managed?

Rejected samples can be flagged with a reason, and recollection requests can be auto-generated for phlebotomists.
