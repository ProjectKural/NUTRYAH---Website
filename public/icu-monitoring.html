FAQs

Can we view bed availability live?

Yes. You can filter by department, status (occupied, on-hold, available), and even link with admission data.

Is this integrated with EMR vitals and monitoring devices?

Yes. Device integrations and vitals are pulled into the ICU panel with alerts configured for abnormal trends.
