FAQs

Can I create orders directly from the EMR?

Yes. All orders can be placed from within the EMR or dashboard with patient context automatically attached.

Are medication orders validated against allergies?

Yes. Nutryah checks for known allergies, drug interactions, and stock availability before confirming orders.
