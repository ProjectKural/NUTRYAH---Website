Doctor Appointment Scheduling

Schedule, filter, and manage appointments with real-time visibility into patient details and clinical workflows — optimized for daily OPD efficiency.

Core Features

  • 📆 Book Appointments with Date & Time Picker
  • 📂 Filter by Department, Doctor, Visit Type, Status
  • 👥 View Patient Details on Click
  • 📋 OPD Tokens and Queue Flow Management
  • 🔄 Auto-Update Status (Confirmed, Arrived, In-Consultation)
  • ⏱️ Time Tracking for Visits

Connected Modules

  • Doctor Dashboard
  • Clinical EMR
  • Visit Details

FAQs

Does Nutryah support recurring appointment slots?

Yes. You can configure recurring slots for specific doctors or departments on a weekly/monthly basis.

Is there a queue management system?

Yes. Each appointment gets a token, and real-time queue status is visible to doctors and front desk staff.
