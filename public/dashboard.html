FAQs

Can I see patient vitals or alerts in real time?

Yes. The dashboard supports live streaming of alerts from EMR, ICU, and pharmacy modules with real-time status changes.

How does it support shift-based handovers?

Shift transitions are captured with handover notes, pending tasks, and patient summaries automatically updated between care teams.

Can doctors take notes directly from the dashboard?

Yes. You can write progress notes, SOAP entries, and access past documentation in one click.

Are alerts real-time?

Yes. Clinical alerts, pending tasks, and lab updates are shown live, enabling proactive patient care.
