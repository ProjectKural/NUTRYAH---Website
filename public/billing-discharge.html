Why Hospitals Choose Nutryah for Discharge

Delays at discharge affect patient satisfaction and operational efficiency. Nutryah’s Billing & Discharge Dashboard offers a centralized platform to finalize bills, update discharge notes, process refunds, and track timelines.

FAQs

Can discharge be initiated by nurses?

Yes. Nutryah allows nurses to initiate clearance while the billing team finalizes charges.

Does it support refund mode selection?

Yes. Refunds can be recorded against original payment modes — UPI, cash, card, or NEFT.
