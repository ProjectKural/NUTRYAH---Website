FAQs

Can doctors sign and lock progress notes?

Yes. Each entry can be signed with user credentials and is locked after finalization, as per NABH and ABDM standards.

Is this EMR mobile-friendly?

Yes. Nutryah EMR is accessible on tablets and smartphones, ensuring care continuity from OPD to ICU and home visits.

Can EMR be used on mobile and tablet?

Yes. Nutryah’s EMR is responsive and accessible from desktop, tablet, or mobile – even during bedside rounds.

Does it support structured history and examination?

Yes. You can use templates, checklists, and structured SOAP note layouts to document findings easily.
