FAQs

Does it support drug-allergy alerts?

Yes. The system checks recorded allergies and warns during prescription entry.

Can I view dispensing history for each patient?

Yes. Each medication action is logged and visible in the patient EMR and pharmacy panel.

Can pharmacists set maximum dose limits?

Yes. Pharmacy admins can define safe dose ranges, restricted drugs, and control prescription access by role.

Does the system alert on allergies or interactions?

Yes. Drug-allergy and drug-drug interaction checks can be configured during medication order placement.
