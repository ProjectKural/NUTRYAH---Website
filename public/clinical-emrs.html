FAQs

Can EMR be used on tablets or mobiles?

Yes. Nutryah EMR is mobile-first, perfect for ward rounds and emergency documentation.

Does it support SOAP and ICD tagging?

Absolutely. All notes support SOAP format and can be tagged with ICD-10, SNOMED, or CPT codes.
