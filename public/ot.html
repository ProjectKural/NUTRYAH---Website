FAQs

Can we track anesthesia timings and meds?

Yes. Anesthesia logs support pre-med, intra-op, and post-anesthesia care unit (PACU) details, including drug usage and vitals monitoring.

Is the system suitable for multi-theatre hospitals?

Absolutely. The module supports multiple OTs with slot conflict prevention, equipment sharing, and surgeon scheduling tools.

Does Nutryah support surgical team assignment?

Yes. Assign surgeon, anesthetist, scrub nurse, and assistant for each surgery with role tagging.

Can OT logs be audited later?

Absolutely. Each procedure is timestamped, user-logged, and compliant with accreditation requirements.
