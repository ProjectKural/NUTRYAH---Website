FAQs

Can I reject a sample with reason tracking?

Yes. Lab staff can mark a sample as rejected and choose predefined reasons (e.g., hemolyzed, insufficient, delayed), with notifications sent to the doctor.

Is this system usable on tablets in collection areas?

Yes. It supports mobile and tablet use for collection counters, bedside sample tagging, and courier transfers.
