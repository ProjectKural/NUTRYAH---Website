FAQs

How does PACS improve radiology workflow?

PACS allows radiologists to upload and retrieve images instantly. Clinicians can review images alongside EMR notes in one place.

Is modality scheduling customizable?

Yes. You can configure modality rooms, slot duration, priority flags, and technician allocation.

Can radiologists view DICOM images in the same system?

Yes. Nutryah‚Äôs RIS includes a built-in PACS viewer that supports DICOM, annotations, and multi-modal image comparisons.

How are final reports managed?

Reports go through a dual-review process, are finalized by senior radiologists, and then auto-notified to the ordering physician.

Can radiologists customize reporting templates?

Yes. Structured reporting templates can be customized for each modality ‚Äî CT, MRI, USG, X-Ray, etc.

How are patients notified of results?

Patients can receive alerts via email or mobile once a report is finalized, and access it securely via the patient portal.
