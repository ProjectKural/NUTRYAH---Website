FAQs

Can we set auto-reminders for medications or nursing tasks?

Yes. Based on patient orders, tasks are auto-scheduled with alerts to respective staff dashboards.

Is handover tracking digital?

Absolutely. Every shift requires acknowledgment and signature via the dashboard before completion.
